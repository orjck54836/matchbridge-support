Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Match Bridge ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Match Bridge mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address and password (encrypted).
  • Profile Information: Photos, nickname, age, gender, and personal description.
  • Location Data: Approximate location to enable nearby matching features.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version for performance improvement.
  • Usage Data: Interactions, matches, and app activity to improve service quality.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide and maintain matching services
  • Enable location-based features
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, or illegal activities
  • Respond to customer support requests

3. Location Usage

Match Bridge uses location data solely to show nearby users and improve matching accuracy. Location data is not shared publicly and is not stored beyond what is necessary for app functionality.

4. Data Storage & Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data. Passwords are encrypted, and access to personal data is restricted. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Data may only be shared:

  • With service providers necessary to operate the app (e.g., hosting providers)
  • If required by law or legal request

6. Account Deletion

You may delete your account at any time within the app settings. Upon deletion:

  • Your profile and photos are permanently removed
  • Your account data is deleted from our servers

7. Children's Privacy

Match Bridge is not intended for users under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
